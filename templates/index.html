Evaluator Instructions

Objective

Your expertise is needed to evaluate computer-generated images. Your goal is to determine which of two images—a "Test" image and a "Comparison" image—is a more faithful and useful representation. Please base your evaluation on key details, texture accuracy, realism, and overall quality.

How to Evaluate

  1. Examine the Image: The main viewer displays the "Test" and "Comparison" images, usually side-by-side.
  2. Use the Tools: This interface is designed for detailed inspection.
    • Zoom: Use your mouse wheel or the + / - buttons.
    • Pan: Click and drag the image to move it around while zoomed in.
    • Reset: Double-click the image or use the ⟲ button to return to the default view.
  3. Rate Comparatively (Section 1): After examining the images, use the first form section to decide which image is better overall, or if they are equal in quality.
  4. Rate Absolutely (Section 2): In the second section, provide a separate 1-to-5 quality score for both the "Test" and "Comparison" images based on their individual merit.
  5. Submit: Click "Save & Next" to record your evaluation and move to the next item. Thank you for your careful analysis!
